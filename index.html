Hey! My name is Kilian and I am a graphic designer and artist based in Naarm/Melbourne. I acknowledge the care, relationship and sovereignty connecting Wurundjeri people to these lands and waters, where I am an uninvited guest. In my exploration of communication, I’m mostly silly but can sometimes be serious, reminding us of our collective belonging. With an interest in the extreme sides of the spectrum and the tension in between, each project becomes a canvas for exploration. For collaborations, get in touch here.
